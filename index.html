Our story

From Human Dental Care to Animal Wellness.

A formula proven on humans, reformulated for the animals you love and the ones you race.

Chika Chika Cool didn't begin as a pet product. Its core formula is built on a patented human gum-treatment recipe — a precise blend of seven medicinal herbs developed to reduce gum inflammation, protect mucosal tissue, and prevent periodontal disease.

When MIMIPET Co., Ltd. began researching oral health solutions for animals, we recognized that the biology behind gum inflammation is not species-specific. The same active compounds — eugenol from clove, glycyrrhizin from licorice, ligustilide from cnidium — work just as effectively in a dog, a horse, or a racing camel.

So we adapted the formula. We removed everything an animal shouldn't ingest. We replaced chemical preservatives with grapefruit seed extract. We made it water-soluble so it can be added to a drinking trough as easily as it can be sprayed directly into a bichon's mouth. The result is a product that brings clinical-grade dental science to animals that have, until now, been left out of the oral care conversation.

Since its official launch in May 2023, Chika Chika Cool has maintained a zero-claim record across both companion and performance animal markets.

The process

12 hours. 70-80°C. No shortcuts.

The extraction process is what separates a working herbal formula from a marketing one.

The active compounds in our seven herbs — eugenol, glycyrrhizin, ligustilide, menthol — are temperature-sensitive. Extract them too hot, and you destroy them. Extract them with ethanol, and you introduce a substance no animal should drink daily.

Chika Chika Cool is produced through controlled low-temperature decoction at 70-80°C for 7 to 12 hours. This range is precise on purpose: it's the sweet spot where the active compounds release into solution without breaking down. The result is a finished product with a measurably higher concentration of working ingredients than fast-extracted alternatives.

Every batch is tested. Every herb is sourced from verified suppliers. And because the extraction yields a stable, water-soluble liquid, no chemical stabilizers are needed downstream.

Application

Stress-free application in four steps.

01

Familiarize

Allow your animal to sniff the nozzle to get comfortable with the herbal scent before any application. For larger animals, introduce the bottle gradually during routine handling sessions.

02

Diluted introduction

Add 5-10 sprays per 100ml of drinking water for the first 1-2 weeks. This builds taste familiarity and delivers preventive benefits with zero stress. Refresh daily.

03

Direct application

Once your animal is comfortable, apply 3 or more sprays evenly across the teeth, gums, and oral cavity. No rinsing required — the formula is safe to swallow.

04

For sensitive animals

If your animal continues to resist direct spraying, simply continue mixing it into drinking water indefinitely — the benefits are equivalent. Some animals never accept direct spraying, and that's okay.

FAQ

Questions, answered.

Is Chika Chika Cool safe to swallow?

Yes. The formula is 100% natural, contains no chemical preservatives, and is intentionally designed to be added to drinking water. There is no rinsing required after direct application.

How long until I see results?

Most pet owners report visibly fresher breath within 3-7 days of daily use. Reduction in plaque and gum inflammation typically becomes visible within 2-3 weeks. For performance animals, the analgesic effect on bit-related discomfort is often immediate.

Will this affect a racing animal's pre-race screening?

No. Chika Chika Cool contains no banned substances. All seven herbal ingredients and the grapefruit seed extract preservative are confirmed as zero-doping. The product is safe for daily use through training and competition.

What's the difference between the 100ml and 1L bottles?

Same formula, different sizes. The 100ml is sized for one or two companion pets and lasts approximately 2-3 months of daily use. The 1L is sized for stable and racing operations and is designed to be added to large drinking troughs across multiple animals.

How do I get my dog or cat to accept the spray?

Start by mixing 5-10 sprays into 100ml of drinking water for the first 1-2 weeks. Once they're used to the herbal taste, transition gradually to direct application. Some pets never fully accept direct spraying — and that's fine. Continued use through drinking water delivers the same benefits.

Can puppies, kittens, or pregnant animals use it?

Yes, the formula is gentle enough for animals of all life stages. As with any new product, we recommend introducing it gradually and consulting your veterinarian if your animal has specific health conditions.

Is it safe for cats specifically?

Yes. Many natural pet products contain ingredients (like xylitol, certain essential oils, or alcohol) that are toxic to cats. Chika Chika Cool contains none of these. The herbal formula is safe for feline use.

How is the product shipped internationally?

We ship globally for both retail and bulk B2B orders. International orders are typically processed within 3-5 business days from our Seoul facility. Contact us for shipping costs to your region and for distributor pricing.

Can I become a distributor in my country?

Yes. We're actively expanding our global distribution network, with a particular focus on the GCC, North America, and Southeast Asia. Bulk pricing and exclusive territory agreements are available. Use the contact form below to start the conversation.

What's the shelf life?

Two years from manufacture date when stored at room temperature in the original sealed bottle. Once opened, use within 12 months for best efficacy. The grapefruit seed extract acts as a natural preservative throughout the shelf life.
